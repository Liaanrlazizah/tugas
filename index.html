~ Daifukumochi ~


Daifukumochi, or daifuku, is a wagashi, consisting of a small round mochi stuffed with a sweet filling, most commonly anko. Daifuku is a popular wagashi in Japan and is often served with green tea. Daifuku comes in many varieties. The most common are white, pale green, or pale pink-colored mochi filled with anko.
